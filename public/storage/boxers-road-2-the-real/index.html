boxer's road 2: the real

what is this?

Boxer's Road 2: The Real is a 2006 video game sequel to Boxer's Road 1 for the PSP.

where to start on pc?

  1. get the emulator PPSSPP or get a PSP/PS Vita
  2. obtain the ROM online (some recommendations)
  3. place the game in a folder that PPSSPP can access
  4. open the game by pressing ctrl + o or find it in the games tab
